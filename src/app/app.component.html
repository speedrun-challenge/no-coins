"No Coins" Speedrun Bounty

Challenge: Get the longest "No Coins" runs for both Subway surfers and Temple Run 2 until September 4th!

Current prize pool: $150
(You want to add to the prize pool? See contact information below)

Participate

Participation is free. Once you have registered, you can submit as many runs on the leaderboards as you want until the challenge ends. Your best (longest) runs will be considered for the bounty.

How to participate:
  1. Submit your speedrun.com username here: Make sure it exactly matches your username.
  2. Submit a run to both Subway surfers "No Coins" (with "No hoverboard/keys") and Temple Run 2 "No Coins" on the speedrun.com leaderboards.

Ranking

Ranking details

For each game, the run times (of all participants) will be ranked 1st, 2nd, 3rd etc. The runner(s) with the lowest rank sum (see example below) will win the bounty.

Example: A Subway Surfers "No Coins" run (for example of 1:35.200) is the 5th best time on the Bounty leaderboard (see below) and a Temple Run 2 "No Coins" run (for example of 1:10.100) is the 8th best time. This gives a rank sum of 5 + 8 = 13. The runner(s) with the lowest rank sum will win the bounty.

Rules

Rule details
  • Only runs submitted before 4th of September, 23:59 BRT (= September 5th 02:59 UTC) will be considered.
  • Runs must be submitted to speedrun.com and therefore follow the respective rules of the game categories. Only runners with a run in both games are eligible for the bounty. The runs must be in the respective "No coins" category, and for Subway Surfers, the run must be of type "No hoverboard/keys".
  • Distribution of prize pool: Below $300: 1 winner (100%), between $300-500: 2 winners (70%-30%), Above $500: 3 winners (50%-30%-20%)
  • Winners will be announced publicly and also be contacted directly via the speedrun.com message system for security measures. Be aware that it may take 2-3 weeks to evaluate the winner, depending on the number of participants.
  • Prizes will be paid out via PayPal or bank wire transfer.
  • Cheating in any form may lead to exclusion of the bounty. Depending on the severity, you will also be reported, for example to speedrun.com. Both is at the discretion of the bounty creator.
